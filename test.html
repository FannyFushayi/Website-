All design work conducted in strict compliance with South African National Standards:

SANS 10142-1: Wiring of Premises (low voltage installations)
SANS 10198: Power cables with extruded insulation systems
SANS 60034 / IEC 60034: Rotating electrical machines (motors)
SANS 60947: Low-voltage switchgear and control gear
SANS 10292: Earthing of low-voltage electrical installations
SANS 1029: Lighting installation standards
NRS 048: Power quality and supply standards

Technical Challenges
