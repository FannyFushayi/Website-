About Me

Hi, I’m Fanny —
an Electrical Engineering graduate, a writer (wannabe at best), and an explorer at the intersection of technology and meaning.

By day, I’m knee-deep in power systems, energy systems, and the evolving logic of machines.
By night (and let’s be honest, often by day too), I write, and read — blending ideas from literature, AI, and the human condition.

This site is both a portfolio and a playground.
One week you’ll find deep dives into tech: control systems, machine learning models, or quantum theory breakdowns.
The next, reflections stitched from Dostoevsky, circuit boards, and whatever existential mystery I’m orbiting that week.

I believe the future needs more than just engineers — it needs curious minds that connect the dots between code and consciousness, systems and stories.

So whether you're here professionally, personally, or just to get lost for a while — welcome.
