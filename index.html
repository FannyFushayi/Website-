About Me

Hi, I'm Fanny.

I’m an Electrical Engineer. By nature, a perpetual overthinker. And by necessity, a writer, though I sometimes resist the title. All three roles are best performed with a coffee in hand.

My days oscillate between power systems, Machine Learning, and the pursuit of clean energy. When the equations blur, you’ll find me reading Dostoevsky or thinking about questions that circuits can’t answer.

This site is my hybrid playground: part engineering portfolio, part philosophical sandbox. One week, it’s machine learning or quantum circuits; the next, it’s thoughts on identity, memory, or why Kafka’s absurdity feels familiar.

I believe the future needs more than technical minds. It needs bridges—between hardware and humanity, algorithms and art, the tangible and the intangible. If engineering is my language, then philosophy, literature, and asking "why?" are my compass.
